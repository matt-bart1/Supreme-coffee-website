ABOUT US

Opened in 1996 in the heart of Manchester, Supreme Coffee had one mission: to bring the very best coffee in the world to the hardworking people of the greatest city on Earth. 22 years later we have succeeded in that mission. Our coffee menu now consists of blends from 36 different countries and we have established ties with farms who practice ethical trading, ensuring the coffee you buy will be helping someone less fortunate.

Our ambitions don't stop there though. We have expanded our expertise into food and other drinks. Our tea selection now consists of blends from 15 countries and our chefs are all michelin star rated.

we are dedicated to our customers and providing the best drink experience we possibly can.

DRINKS

Coffee is what we do best.
We have traveled the globe to find the very best coffee from the most ethical farmers we can. From Colombia to Australia we at Supreme Coffee are passionate about our Java. From smoky, rich coffee to fruity refreshing teas Supreme has got your caffeine cravings covered.

FOOD

Supreme Coffee is not just about drinks though. We have a very tasty menu of food as well. From light bites to meals, from the best meat dishes to the widest choice of vegan dishes in Manchester our chefs are michelin rated and nationally renowned. You can be sure that Supreme offers something for everyone

ALCOHOL

Feeling frisky ? If you are looking for something more than coffee or tea Supreme is fully licensed to serve alcohol, and we offer a very tempting cocktail and special drinks menu. Fancy a cosmo ? we got that. Mojitos ? no problem.

All drinks are available after 1pm
